# Call Script

## Opening

"I know your business and was looking at your website from the perspective of a property manager on mobile. The company itself looks established and credible. The opportunity is that the homepage is not showing that credibility as clearly as it could."

## UX Score Talk Track

"The current site has good substance: commercial services, snow removal, service since 1958, 24/7 availability, memberships, and a client portal. But on mobile, the hero text clips and the first screen does not immediately say: we are a dependable commercial property partner."

"The redesign concept moves the strongest proof to the top and gives visitors clearer paths: request a commercial quote, view snow and ice services, or access the client portal."

## Offer

"I can rebuild this as a focused mobile-first site for $1,950. I would keep your current site live while building, then launch only after approval. If you want me to host and maintain it, that can be a simple monthly plan."

Suggested maintenance:

"Hosting, small edits, uptime checks, and basic reporting would be $149/month."

## Objections

"We already have a website."

Response:
"Definitely. I am not saying you do not have one. I am saying your website has strong content, but the mobile presentation is not doing the business justice. The rebuild is about making existing credibility easier to see and act on."

"We do not want a big project."

Response:
"That is exactly why I would keep the first scope tight: homepage, core service structure, quote path, and mobile cleanup. No disruption to the current site while the new one is being built."

"Can we update it ourselves?"

Response:
"Yes. If that is important, we can choose a setup that supports that. For the prototype I used static code because it is fast and clean, but production could be WordPress if direct editing matters."
