# Twin Cities Contracting Services Pitch Brief

## Business

Twin Cities Contracting Services Inc. is a Bloomington roofing contractor serving the Twin Cities metro. The current site emphasizes roofing experience since 1964, roof replacement, roof installation, roof inspection, residential shingle roofing, and phone-first service at `(952) 405-6201`.

## Why This Prospect

The company appears established and credible, but the mobile homepage can do a better job presenting that credibility quickly. The current site has strong trust signals, service depth, and local SEO content, but the first screen is dense and the captured mobile hero headline overflows the viewport.

## Current UX Opportunity

- The mobile hero headline is long and visually crowded.
- The primary action is present, but it competes with SEO-heavy headline copy.
- Trust proof is available but could be surfaced earlier and more compactly.
- Services can be organized as homeowner decision paths rather than long content blocks.
- The company identity can be preserved while making the site feel more current.

## Proposed Redesign

The prototype reframes the homepage around homeowner action:

- H1: "Roofing since 1964."
- Primary CTA: Call for roofing service.
- Secondary CTA: Request free inspection.
- Proof strip: since 1964, Bloomington office, residential roofing, free inspection/service path.
- Service cards for replacement, installation, inspection, and shingle roofing.
- Project imagery pulled from the public site.
- Low-friction concept form clearly marked as demo-only.

## Offer

Suggested early-case-study offer:

- $1,950 flat homepage/site rebuild.
- Optional $149/month for hosting, maintenance, uptime checks, small edits, and basic reporting.

Scope should stay focused:

- Homepage redesign.
- Mobile-first conversion cleanup.
- Contact/inspection request flow.
- Preserve SEO depth and existing URL equity in a production migration.
- Existing site remains live until launch.
