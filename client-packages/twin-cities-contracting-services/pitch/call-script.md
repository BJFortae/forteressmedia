# Call Script

## Opener

Hi, this is Brenden. I had a quick website idea for Twin Cities Contracting Services and wanted to see who handles the website or marketing.

## If They Ask What It Is

I noticed the site already has strong trust signals: roofing since 1964, Bloomington/Twin Cities positioning, and clear residential roofing services. I put together a private homepage concept that makes those strengths easier to see on mobile and gives homeowners a faster call / inspection request path.

## Core Pitch

This is not a criticism of the business. The reputation is the valuable part. The opportunity is making the website feel as established and easy to trust as the company itself.

## Discovery Questions

- Are most of your leads phone calls, form fills, referrals, or repeat customers?
- Do you want the site to push inspections, replacements, storm damage help, or general roofing calls first?
- Do you update the site internally, or does someone else manage it?
- Are there claims we should be careful about, like licensing, insurance, warranties, or preferred materials?

## Offer

For early case-study work, I can rebuild the homepage for a flat $1,950, with optional $149/month hosting, maintenance, uptime checks, and small edits. The current site would stay live until a replacement is approved.

## Close

Would it be helpful if I sent over the private preview and a before/after mobile screenshot?
