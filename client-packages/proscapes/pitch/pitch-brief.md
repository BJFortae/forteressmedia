# ProScapes Pitch Brief

## Business

ProScapes is a Twin Cities commercial grounds maintenance, landscaping, and snow removal company based around Eagan / Minneapolis-St. Paul. The current site indicates service since 1958, 24/7/365 availability, a client portal, and commercial/residential service lines.

## Why This Prospect

The business appears established and operationally credible, but the website does not fully express that credibility on mobile. The current mobile hero clips text, the site has a certificate verification issue in automated fetches, and the strongest trust proof is buried below the first impression.

## Current UX Problem

Current site issues:
- Mobile hero text clips horizontally.
- Commercial and residential audiences are mixed without a clear primary path.
- "Get Started" is generic for property managers who likely want a quote, snow response, or service validation.
- Strong proof points are underused: since 1958, 24/7/365, client portal, memberships, and communication software.

## Proposed Redesign

The prototype reframes the homepage around commercial reliability:
- H1 focused on landscapes, snow response, and property care since 1958.
- Snow fleet image as the hero proof asset.
- Primary CTA: Request Commercial Quote.
- Secondary CTA: Snow & Ice Services.
- Proof strip directly below hero.
- Services organized around property-manager needs.
- Short quote form with property type and services needed.

## Offer

Suggested early-case-study offer:
- $1,950 flat homepage/site rebuild.
- Optional $149/month for hosting, maintenance, uptime checks, small edits, and basic reporting.

Scope should stay focused:
- Homepage redesign.
- Core service path structure.
- Contact/quote form setup.
- Mobile-first performance and SSL cleanup.
- Existing site remains live until launch.
